From here you may:

1.  Continue Editing a Paid Fund Raiser registration which you have already begun to enter, but have not yet filed.   To do so, please click the “Edit Existing” button and follow the instructions and prompts which will be given on the upcoming pages.

 2. Begin to enter a new Paid Fund Raiser registration for a year which has not yet been filed.  To do so, please click on the “Create New Registration” button and follow the instructions and prompts which will be provided as you progress through the registration process.
